Our Mission

At Seventy7Sports, we aim to inspire active futures for the next generation, using sports as a catalyst of empowerment to enable kids discover their inner strength & athletic abilities.

Our Vision

To globally establish and foster the idea that through sport we can UNITE, CHALLENGE, OVERCOME, and INSPIRE the next generation towards a future of inclusivity, resilience, and boundless possibilities, empowering them to lead with passion, purpose, and excellence.

Our Youths Sports Philosophy
All children in our community will have the opportunity to participate in youth sports programs. Programs will be offered at a reasonable cost while providing quality facilities and equipment. All staff within the program will act as role models, mentors and facilitators. All participants in our youth sports programs shall be treated fairly regardless of disability, religion, gender or origin. Practices and games should be an enjoyable, challenging and learning experience for all.

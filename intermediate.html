Overview

Seventy7Multipurpose Camp

The multi-sport camp offers campers the opportunity to play a wide variety of sports and games. Campers will get to play traditional sports like basketball, and football but will also get the chance to play sports they have never tried before such as Flag-football, Ultimate Frisbee and more. The objective of the multi-sport camp is to introduce campers to as many sports as possible - to see what they would like to pursue further. Coaches will help campers learn the basics of each sport while creating a fun environment for all.

Football Camp

Participants learn the art of the game through activities that emphasize ball control, passing, dribbling, and teamwork. Whether it's the Soccer Tots mastering the basics, Hawks developing strategies, or Titans focusing on advanced tactics, football at the Multipurpose Camp is about more than just the sport—it’s about growing as a team player and leader.

Basketball Camp

Basketball at Seventy7Sports encourages agility, precision, and confidence. Younger players enjoy dribbling and shooting drills, while the older groups focus on court awareness, passing strategies, and game play. All age groups are encouraged to embrace the spirit of collaboration and perseverance.

Volleyball Camp

Our volleyball program introduces children to the fundamentals of the sport, such as serving, setting, and spiking. Soccer Tots enjoy fun games that enhance motor skills, while Hawks delve into basic formations and teamwork. Titans advance to competitive play, focusing on game strategies, coordination, and leadership.

Camp Activities

Football Camp

Skill Dribbling Drills

Participants practice navigating through cones and obstacles to enhance ball control and footwork precision.

Passing and Positioning Games

Players engage in passing relays and small-sided games to develop accurate passing and spatial awareness.

Mini Goal Challenges

A fast-paced activity where participants aim to score into smaller goals, focusing on shooting technique and decision-making.

Tactical Scrimmages

Players compete in structured matches with specific tactical objectives to improve teamwork and strategy.

Camp Activities

Basketball Camp

Dribbling Relays

Players race while dribbling through cones, improving ball-handling skills under pressure.

Shooting Stars Competitions

Participants practice shooting from different court positions in a timed challenge to enhance accuracy.

Passing Precision Drills

A group activity focusing on chest passes, bounce passes, and overhead passes to build control and timing.

Half-Court Scrimmages

Small-sided games where players focus on applying learned skills, teamwork, and offensive strategies.

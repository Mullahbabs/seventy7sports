Overview

Seventy7Multisports Camp

The multi-sport camp offers campers the opportunity to play a wide variety of sports and games. Campers will get to play traditional sports like basketball, and football but will also get the chance to play sports they have never tried before such as Flag-football, Ultimate Frisbee and more. The objective of the multi-sport camp is to introduce campers to as many sports as possible - to see what they would like to pursue further. Coaches will help campers learn the basics of each sport while creating a fun environment for all.

Camp Activities

Basketball Camp

Basketball at Seventy7Sports encourages agility, precision, and confidence. Younger players enjoy dribbling and shooting drills, while the older groups focus on court awareness, passing strategies, and game play. All age groups are encouraged to embrace the spirit of collaboration and perseverance.

Basketball Buddies (4-6)

Introducing little ones to the joy of basketball with interactive games that develop hand-eye coordination, basic dribbling, and passing skills. The focus is on fun and fostering an early love for the game.

Rising Hoopsters (7-10)

Build confidence with drills that teach foundational skills like shooting, ball control, and teamwork. Campers engage in friendly games to learn court awareness and basic strategies.

Elite Dunkers (11-15)

Take your game to the next level with advanced techniques in dribbling, defensive maneuvers, and offensive play. This camp emphasizes skill refinement, teamwork, and competitive play.

Volleyball Camp

Our volleyball program introduces children to the fundamentals of the sport, such as serving, setting, and spiking. Soccer Tots enjoy fun games that enhance motor skills, while Hawks delve into basic formations and teamwork. Titans advance to competitive play, focusing on game strategies, coordination, and leadership.

Volley Starters (4-6)

A playful introduction to volleyball through light exercises, balloon volleyball, and simplified gameplay. Focuses on developing motor skills, coordination, and a sense of teamwork.

Net Explorers (7-10)

Learn the essential skills of volleyball, including setting, serving, and spiking. Campers practice through drills and games that encourage collaboration and communication.

Court Commanders (11-15)

Advanced training for older campers, focusing on complex strategies, positioning, and rotations. Players engage in scrimmages and team-building exercises to develop confidence in competitive settings.

Lawn Tennis Camp

Unleash your inner champion at our Lawn Tennis Camps! Perfect for players of all experience levels, these camps focus on skill development, agility, and strategic play. Through engaging activities and expert coaching, young athletes will build confidence, foster sportsmanship, and enjoy the thrill of the game.

Tennis Tykes (4-6)

An engaging introduction to tennis with games and drills that teach basic racquet handling, ball control, and coordination. The emphasis is on having fun and staying active.

Baseline Builders (7-10)

Focused training on forehand, backhand, and serving techniques. Campers participate in match simulations to develop their skills in a supportive environment.

Grand Slam Heroes (11-15)

Advanced gameplay strategies, including topspin, volleys, and footwork drills. Players refine their skills and learn the mental aspects of tennis to perform under pressure.

Overview

Seventy7Football Camps

At Seventy7Sports Camp, we believe in creating a dynamic and nurturing environment where kids can develop not only their football skills but also valuable life lessons. Our football camps are designed to cater to young athletes across three distinct categories: Soccer Tots, Hawks, and Titans. Each program is tailored to meet the unique developmental and skill needs of these age groups, ensuring a fun and enriching experience for every camper.

Camp Activities

Soccer-Tots Camp

The Soccer Tots camp is all about introducing young players to the joys of football in a fun and safe environment. Through simple and engaging activities, kids build foundational skills and develop a love for the game.

Fun Ball Dribbling

Young players navigate through colorful cones, focusing on controlling the ball while exploring movement in all directions.

Circle Time Passing

Participants sit in a circle and pass the ball to one another, learning the basics of passing and receiving while having fun with their peers.

Mini Goal Scoring

Children take turns kicking the ball into small-sized goals, celebrating each attempt with cheers and high-fives, fostering confidence and enthusiasm.

Ball and Balance Games

Kids balance soccer balls on their feet or knees while standing still, improving their focus and coordination.

Operational Objectives

* Control, Passing, Shooting & Turns * Running with the ball * Fundamental motor skills
* Spatial awareness * Teamwork * Confidence and self-esteem

Hawks Camp

The Hawks camp builds on fundamental skills while introducing basic tactics and teamwork. Activities are designed to be slightly more challenging to keep kids engaged and motivated.

Small-Sided Matches

Kids play in teams of 3v3 or 4v4, learning positioning, teamwork, and decision-making in a friendly, competitive setup.

Target Passing Drills

Participants pass the ball into specific targets or zones, improving their accuracy and control under guided instruction.

Defender Challenge

Children take turns kicking the ball into small-sized goals, celebrating each attempt with cheers and high-fives, fostering confidence and enthusiasm.

Relay Races With A Ball

Teams compete in dribbling relay races, focusing on speed, control, and teamwork to reach the finish line.

Titans Camp

The Titans camp emphasizes advanced skills, tactical awareness, and leadership development, preparing players for competitive environments.

Tactical Scrimmages

Players participate in structured games with specific goals, such as maintaining possession or executing set plays, enhancing strategic thinking.

Skill Mastery Drills

Focused drills on advanced techniques like stepovers, nutmegs, and quick turns help players sharpen their individual skills.

Leadership Challenges

Players are assigned leadership roles during games and drills, developing communication and decision-making abilities.

Fitness And Endurance Trainings

Participants engage in high-intensity workouts combined with ball exercises, building stamina and preparing them for competitive matches.

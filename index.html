About Us

Seventy7Sports

At Seventy7Sports, we are passionate about fueling your love for sports. Whether you’re a professional athlete or a weekend warrior, our mission is to provide you with the gear, resources, and inspiration you need to perform at your best. From cutting-edge equipment and apparel to expert training advice, we bring together the best in sports innovation to help you reach new heights With a commitment to quality and performance.

  • Mission

Our Mission

At Seventy7Sports, we aim to inspire active futures for the next generation, using sports as a catalyst for empowerment to enable kids discover their inner strength & athletic abilities.

Our Vision

To globally establish and foster the idea that through sport we can UNITE, CHALLENGE, OVERCOME, and INSPIRE the next generation towards a future of inclusivity, resilience, and boundless possibilities, empowering them to lead with passion, purpose, and excellence.

Integrity & Values

We are committed to fostering a culture of honesty, accountability, and respect in all aspects of our operations. Whether working with athletes, partners, or communities, we hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards to ensure transparency and fairness.

Our camps

Seventy7 Football Camp


Seventy7Sports Camp provides a well-rounded and engaging sports environment for kids of all ages. In addition to the multi-sport focus, we also aim to embed character education, teaching kids about sportsmanship, inclusion, teamwork, and friendship.

Soccer-tots Camp

Our focus is physical literacy and exciting games to build confidence and teach the fundamentals of soccer. Within this curriculum players will cover ball familiarization, small group dynamics, motor skill development and social interaction, all with maximum participation in a fun oriented environment.

Hawks Camp

This program enhances player’s technical development and skill building in a stimulating and positive environment. Players are challenged to consider basic tactical components at an age appropriate level, including attacking and defending principles.

Titans Camps

This program challenges the players technically and tactically in an enjoyable environment, through skill development and small-sided games. Players are coached to understand when and where to use key techniques along with decision- making skills to achieve greater success.

Our camps

Seventy7Multisports Camp


The multi-sport camp offers campers the opportunity to play a wide variety of sports and games. Campers will get to play traditional sports like basketball, and football but will also get the chance to play sports they have never tried before such as Flag-football, Ultimate Frisbee and more.

Basketball Camps

Basketball at Seventy7Sports encourages agility, precision, and confidence. Younger players enjoy dribbling and shooting drills, while the older groups focus on court awareness, passing strategies, and game play. All age groups are encouraged to embrace the spirit of collaboration and perseverance.

Volleyball Camps

This program enhances player’s technical development and skill building in a stimulating and positive environment. Players are challenged to consider basic tactical components at an age appropriate level, including attacking and defending principles.

Frisbee Camps

This program challenges the players technically and tactically in an enjoyable environment, through skill development and small-sided games. Players are coached to understand when and where to use key techniques along with decision- making skills to achieve greater success.

Lawn Tennis Camps

Our Lawn Tennis Camps provide an exciting and structured training experience for players of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the fundamentals or an advanced player aiming to refine your techniques, our expert coaches are dedicated to helping you improve your game.
